01 — Brand Identity

The studio behind
every studio.

Bukarrum is a B2B2C SaaS platform that empowers creative studios — recording rooms, rehearsal spaces, photo studios — with a fully branded public booking page and a powerful admin dashboard. We sit invisibly behind every booking, making both the studio owner and their customers feel like the platform was built just for them.

01
Infrastructure, not interruption
We disappear into the studio's experience. The booking flow feels native to their brand, not like a third-party tool.
02
Creative at the core
Our users are musicians, photographers, videographers. Our brand must feel at home in the same world they work in.
03
Simple on top, powerful below
End customers get effortless booking. Studio owners get deep control over every resource and configuration.
Mission: Make professional studio booking feel as natural as sending a message — for studios across Chile and LATAM.

Vision: Become the invisible infrastructure that powers creative commerce in Latin America.

Color usage principles
Lime is earned, not given
Only primary CTAs, active nav items, selected states, and key brand moments use Lime. Do not use it for decoration.
Dark is the canvas
Design dark-first. Light mode should feel like a well-lit studio, not an overexposed photo. Off-white backgrounds, not pure white.

05 — Voice & Tone

Confident.
Not corporate.

Bukarrum speaks like a knowledgeable friend who runs studios — straightforward, warm, with a slight edge. We never over-explain. We're Chilean, we know our market, and we talk like it.

✓ Do this
"Your recording room is booked. Show up, create."
Direct, assumes competence, ends with purpose.
✗ Not this
"Congratulations! Your reservation has been successfully processed and confirmed."
Redundant. Patronizing. Sounds like a bank.
✓ Do this
"Sala A · $15.000/hr · Available from 14:00"
Dense with value. Let the info do the talking.
✗ Not this
"We are pleased to offer you access to our state-of-the-art recording facilities."
Passive, wordy, no personality.
✓ Do this
"Something went wrong with this slot. Try another or contact the studio."
Honest about failure, immediately helpful.
✗ Not this
"We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience this may have caused you."
Stalling instead of helping.
Tone modifiers by context
Booking flow
Calm, frictionless, concise. Every word earns its place. Zero unnecessary confirmation steps.
Admin dashboard
Functional, precise. Labels over tooltips. Familiar patterns. We respect that studio owners are busy.
Marketing / landing
More energy, slight edge. We can make a statement. Chilean directness, global ambition.
06 — Audience

Two users.
One platform.

Bukarrum serves two distinct audiences with different needs, mental models, and moments in the product. We design for both without confusing either.

Tenant (Studio Owner)
The Studio Operator
Runs a recording studio, rehearsal space, or photo studio in Chile. Wants to stop managing bookings via WhatsApp. Needs control over their resources, availability, pricing, and clients — without needing a developer.
Self-employed 1–5 staff WhatsApp-heavy ops Tech-cautious Value-driven
End Customer (Booker)
The Creative
A musician, band, photographer, or content creator looking to reserve a space. They'll interact with the studio's branded Bukarrum page — and may not know Bukarrum exists at all.
Mobile-first Spontaneous planner Price-aware Wants speed Trust via aesthetics
B2B2C brand logic
Bukarrum's brand is primarily visible to tenants (studio owners) in the admin dashboard and during onboarding. End customers experience the studio's brand, powered silently by Bukarrum. This is intentional — our best marketing is an invisible, seamless booking experience.
